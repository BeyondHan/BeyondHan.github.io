#### Vision-Tactile Fusion Fruit Sorting Robot System
**Master's Core Research | National Natural Science Foundation of China | 2023 - Present**

Designed and implemented a closed-loop perception-decision-execution robotic system integrating a self-developed 3D-printed pneumatic soft gripper, magnetic tactile sensor array (1x4 Hall-effect), deep learning perception (CNN-BiLSTM-Attention), and UR5 manipulator control. Achieved 99.26% fruit classification accuracy, 96.88% kiwi maturity grading, and 97.77% end-to-end sorting success rate.

*Keywords: Soft Robotics, Tactile Sensing, Deep Learning, RGB-D Vision, UR5, Eye-to-Hand Calibration*

#### Embodied Intelligence Robot Arm Full-Stack Control System
**Independent Open-Source Project | [GitHub](https://github.com/BeyondHan/Embodied-AI-Robot-Arm) | 2024 - 2025**
